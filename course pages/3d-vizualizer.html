ArchViz - 3dsMax - VRay

Architectural Visualization with 3dsMax and Vray

3D Visualization is used in various fields such as Advertising, Architecture, Interior Design and many more... This course specializes in the creation of Photorealistic 3D Stills and Walkthroughs for Architecture and Interior Design with Autodesk 3dsMax and VRay along with some support softwares.

  • Duration

    60 Days - 120 Hours

  • Schedule

    5 Days a Week - 2 Hours a Day

  • Training Mode

    Online / Offline

Learning Outcomes

After completing this course ...

  • You will be able to create stunning, photorealistic 3D Visualizations.

  • You will be able to draft your own ideas with Autocad with accuracy.

  • You will learn to prepare complex Autocad Drafts for 3D Visualization.

  • You will learn to create complex 2D Shapes, 2D Patterns and use them to create 3D Objects.

  • You will be able to create beautiful 2D Textures and Maps for 3D Visualization.

  • You will firmly grasp the core fundamentals of 3dsmax as a tool for Architectural Visualization.

  • You will learn to create accurate and realistic 3D models.

  • You will learn a unique technique that we call as 'Hybrid Modelling Technique' to rapidly create Architectural entities.

  • You will learn to mimic real-world lights with 3dsmax.

  • You will gain a deep understanding of Natural and Artificial Light simulation in 3dsmax.

  • You will learn to create various materials that look extraordinarily real.

  • You will gain in-depth knowledge of different mapping techniques.

  • You will learn the most important concepts of Photography and 3dsmax Physical Camera.

  • You will gain in-depth knowledge of render engines such as Arnold & Vray.

  • You will learn how to use Arnold and Vray for speedy test renders.

  • You will be able to control the render quality like a Pro.

  • You will gain a deep understanding of the 'Render Time Vs Render Quality' equation.

  • You will be able to create extraordinary renders in the shortest amount of time.

  • You will learn how to post-prosess and finalize rendered images.

  • You will learn how to keep post-processing at the minimum and make Arnold and Vray do the heavy lifting.
