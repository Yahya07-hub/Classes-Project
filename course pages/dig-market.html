Become a Digital Marketer in 120 Days

In view of the report of Global Data, the Indian online business market is pushing to 7 trillion rupees by 2023 as a result of lockdowns. Thus, digital marketing is a futuristic career which pays an entry level digital marketer an average salary of ₹3.0 lakhs to ₹4.0 lakhs per annum.

Digital Marketing

Designing, Marketing & Analysing Tools of the 21st Century

Digital marketing is a broad field, including attracting customers via email, content marketing, search platforms, social media, and more. An entirely new endeavour that requires a new way of approaching customers and new ways of understanding how customers behave compared to traditional marketing. In this course, you will learn how the digital marketing world works and how to make it work according to your requirements.

  • Duration

    60 Days - 120 Hours

  • Schedule

    5 Days a Week - 2 Hours a Day

  • Training Mode

    Online / Offline

Learning Outcomes

After completing this course ...

  • You will learn to create modern, responsive websites

  • You will learn the concept of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Search Engine Marketing (SEM)

  • You will learn to create eye catching graphics and videos contents for social media and content marketing campaigns.

  • You will learn to create promotional messages for email marketing campaigns.

  • You will learn to monitor, maintain and troubleshoot your website’s presence in Google Search results.

  • You will learn to access your website traffic details as well as your google ad campaigns details on Google Analytics tools and further use them for better results.
