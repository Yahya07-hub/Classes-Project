Our Teaching Methodology

In today's world, information is available at our fingertips. Education is no different. But sadly, knowledge acquired haphazardly cannot be applied in real-world situations.

At aakrutis, we have a modern syllabus, designed and taught by professionals in a step-by-step manner.

We believe in a task-oriented teaching methodology that makes our students stay at the top in today's competitive environment.

The Learning App

We have developed a 'Learning App' that acts as a digital classroom for our students.

Students are given login credentials for the App upon enrollment. Once logged-in, students can view their previous recorded lectures, download lecture & practice files, upload their assignments, get course related notifications and also contact their faculties for further guidance.

The App can be accessed with a Mobile, a Tablet, a Laptop or a Desktop PC as well.
